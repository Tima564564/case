# Development Roadmap

## Phase 0: Foundation

- Monorepo, Docker, Postgres, Prisma.
- Telegram auth and JWT.
- Premium mobile-first UI shell.
- Basic admin role and audit shape.

## Phase 1: MVP

- Case list and case details.
- Weighted case opening.
- Roulette animation.
- Inventory and sell-to-balance.
- Wallet ledger.
- Live feed through Socket.IO.
- Daily reward.
- EN/RU/UA copy structure.
- Admin case and drop management.

## Phase 2: PvP And Upgrade

- Upgrade chance calculation and settlement.
- Case battle lobby.
- Battle spectator mode.
- Winner-takes-all settlement.
- Provably fair reveal screen.
- Battle history and player stats.

## Phase 3: Crypto Economy

- Crypto deposit provider.
- Withdrawal review workflow.
- Payment webhook idempotency.
- Risk checks for withdrawals.
- Locked balance for active battles and pending withdrawals.

## Phase 4: Scale And Protection

- Redis adapter and distributed locks.
- Queue workers for payments and battles.
- Fraud scoring and manual review tools.
- Advanced admin analytics.
- CDN/R2 media pipeline.
- Error monitoring and product analytics.

## Phase 5: Growth

- Referral campaigns.
- Seasonal leaderboards.
- Battle tournaments.
- Loyalty tiers.
- Personalized offers.
